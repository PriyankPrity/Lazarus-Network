Purpose

The real motivation for this project is to allow users access a Safer Internet wherever they are and whichever Device they use (From Smartphones to IoT Devices).

  • we built a Decentralized VPN Services Marketplace where a common standard in the network would be followed across multiple service providers or self hosted solutions.
  • We learnt about a lot of challenges in connectivity, security and most importantly privacy in VPN Systems.
  • Given the isolation currently being experienced due to the COVID-19 pandemic, almost all of the people are working from home and not all of them have access to the enterprise firewall in their homes.
  • The most vulnerable are the startups and small businesses who would be soft targets for phishing attacks, ransomware, malware or internet viruses.
  • Hence we decided to work on a secure firewall which filters the internet based on blacklisted Websites/IP/domains and only allows the users to access safe websites.
  • Also SMEs are looking to rapidly transition to Web3 & DApp Driven Ecosystem to offer additional value add to their existing business operations.
